This is a graph of Annual Household Income in the United States over time from 1990 to 2021.

However, besides income, an important issue to track is average consumer expenditure. The difference shows how much the average person has leftover.

The size of each circle is determined by that difference of income - expenditure.

Finally to see the data through another dimension, we use the color red and blue to indicate whether the difference of income - expenditure is greater than or less than the mean value over the years.
